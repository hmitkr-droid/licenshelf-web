The Process

From license to royalty,
in four steps.

No lawyers, no negotiations, no paperwork. Here's the entire journey.

01

We collect licenses

Licenshelf negotiates directly with IP holders — gaming studios, anime producers, independent artists — to build a curated license library.

02

Sellers access the library

Inside Printify or Gelato, sellers browse the Licenshelf library, pick a licensed image, and apply it to any product — no extra steps.

03

Royalty auto-calculated

When a product sells, our system automatically calculates and adds the license fee. No invoices, no manual payments.

04

IP holders get paid

Rights holders receive periodic royalty payments — from a market segment they previously couldn't reach at all.
